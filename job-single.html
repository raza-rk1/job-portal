Product Designer

Puma New York City Full Time

Job Overview

We are seeking a talented Product Designer to join our team. As a Product Designer, you will be responsible for designing and developing innovative products and solutions that meet the needs of our clients. You will work closely with cross-functional teams, including product managers, engineers, and UX designers, to ensure that the product is functional, user-friendly, and visually appealing.

Responsibilities

  • Developing and presenting new product designs based on market research and consumer insights.
  • Collaborating with product development teams to ensure designs are executed properly and meet technical specifications.
  • Creating and maintaining design documentation, including sketches, technical drawings, and specifications.
  • Staying up to date on market trends and competitor products to ensure PUMA's designs remain competitive and relevant.
  • Managing multiple projects simultaneously, prioritizing tasks, and meeting project deadlines.

Education + Experience

  • A bachelor's degree in fashion design, product design, or a related field.
  • Several years of experience designing footwear or apparel for a fashion or sportswear brand.
  • Strong knowledge of design software, including Adobe Creative Suite and 3D modeling software.
  • Excellent communication, collaboration, and project management skills.
  • A passion for sports and fashion, and a desire to create innovative and functional products that meet the needs of consumers.
